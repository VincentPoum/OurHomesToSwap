La PoumerolePictures and description

The most peaceful !


No worry, no rush, no noise except the song of the birds and sometimes the mooing of a cow.



The house


Our home is a charming refurbished vintage home. (XIX century)

Open kitchen/dining room, livingroom, 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, 2 toilets.

Very large garden and terrasse with view on the volcanoes in the distance.



The surroundings


La Poumerole is a hamlet of 3 houses, situated in the Auvergne countryside, an hour from Clermont-Ferrand, in the centre of France.

As the two others houses are not occupied most of the time, it is the perfect place to enjoy quiet country life.



What to do ?


Sitting on the terrasse and enjoying the calm and the view.

Walking in the neighbourhood of the house ( trails guides at home)

Swimming in the (15 minutes drive) Lac des Fades-Besserves

Fishing in l’Etang de Chancelade (famous for its carps and pikes)

Canoeing on the Sioule river

Testing the famous Auvergne’s cheeses : Saint-Nectaire, Cantal, Fourme d’Ambert, Bleu d’Auvergne


Visiting

  • The regional Nature Parks (parc des volcans d’Auvergne)

  • The Méandre de Queuille

  • The Gorges de la Sioule

  • The hotsprings resorts of Auvergne (Châtel-Guyon, Royat, Vichy …)

  • The city of Clermont-Ferrand and its cathedral Notre-Dame-de-l’Assomption

  • The valley of la Voueize, the old town of Chambon and its abbey-church

  • Aubusson (one hour drive) and its Tapestry museum
