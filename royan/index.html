RoyanPictures and description

The most enjoyable seaside place...



The house


Pleasant house very close to the beach and casino , but nevertheless very quiet surrounding, ideal for seaside holidays.

Fully-equipped kitchen, livingroom, 3 bedrooms ( 2 double, 1 with 4 bunk beds for children) 2 bathrooms, 2 toilets.

Large terrasse, garden.



The surroundings


The house, situated in Vaux-sur-mer, is very close to the seaside resort of Royan, at the mouth of the Gironde, between Bordeaux and La Rochelle.

The beautiful beach of Pontaillac is at only 5 minutes walking, different stores as well.



What to do ?


Enjoying the pleasures of the ocean, beach, surfing, sailing …

Relaxing on the terrasse

Visiting …


  • History :

    • Rochefort with its superb rope factory
    • The ancient town of La Rochelle
    • The roman ruins of Saintes
    • The very old lighthouse of Cordouan
    • The ancient roman churches of Saintonge
    • The ancient fortress of Brouage
    • The turn of the century houses of Royan with their unique architecture ...

  • Natural scenery :

    • The Côte de Beauté from the mouth of the Seudre to Talmont (beautiful village at the mouth of Gironde) Saint- Palais, Saint-Georges-de-Didonne, Meschers and its caves dwellings …
    • The forêt de la Coubre, its lighthouse and wild beaches
    • The peaceful Marais Poitevin and its canals
    • The islands of Ré and Oléron

    • and the wonderful zoo of La Palmyre

  • Gastronomy :

    • The delicious oysters and mussels from Marennes
    • The famous Cognac and Pineau des Charentes
    • The Bordeaux vineyards only a short drive away
